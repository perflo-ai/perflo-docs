Perflo is a regulated money platform. Identity verification is required before you can hold balances, trade, create cards, or send and receive payments. This protects you and keeps the platform compliant.

## Individuals (KYC)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Provide your details">
    Legal name, date of birth, address, and nationality.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload a document">
    A valid government-issued ID such as a passport, national ID card, or driving licence.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Take a selfie">
    A short liveness check confirms the document belongs to you.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Most verifications complete within minutes. If a manual review is needed, we notify you in the app and by email.

## Businesses (KYB)

Business verification covers the company and the people behind it:

- Company registration documents and registered address
- Details of directors and ultimate beneficial owners (UBOs)
- The individual operating the account completes personal KYC as well

Once approved, the business gets access to business bank accounts, first-party and third-party pay-ins and payouts, cards, and agent access under the business profile.

## Why some actions stay locked

Certain features and limits depend on your verification level and your country. If a feature is not available to you, the app says so before you start. Sanctions screening and transaction monitoring run continuously on all accounts, for human and agent activity alike.

<Note>
Your documents are used for verification and compliance only. See [Security](/reference/security) for how account access and permissions work.
</Note>
