**Goal:** money in your Perflo cash balance. You need a [verified account](/getting-started/verify-identity) first. All four methods land in the same balance, and from there you can buy assets, trade, fund cards, or pay out.

| Method | Best for | Typically arrives |
|:---|:---|:---|
| Bank transfer to your virtual account | Salaries, larger amounts, getting paid by others | Instant to same day on local rails; your bank's speed applies |
| Debit or credit card | First top-up, speed | Instantly |
| Recurring bank funding | Keeping a balance topped up automatically | On your schedule, or when your balance runs low |
| Crypto deposit | Moving in existing USDT/USDC | Seconds to minutes, depending on the network |

## 1. Bank transfer to your virtual account

Once verified, you can create virtual bank accounts with local account details and send money to yourself like a normal bank transfer. Accounts are live in ten currencies today, including AED, USD, EUR, GBP, BRL, MXN, COP, NGN, and PHP, each on its local rails (for example instant AED payments, SEPA Instant for EUR, Faster Payments for GBP, PIX for BRL). Third-party pay-ins are supported, so a client or marketplace can pay you directly into your account.

**You'll see:** the transfer land in your cash balance with an activity-feed entry, and a notification when it arrives. The full currency list is on [Supported currencies and assets](/reference/supported-currencies), and rails per currency on [Bank accounts](/cards-banking/bank-accounts).

## 2. Debit or credit card

Top up instantly with a debit or credit card. The exact amount, currency conversion, and any card processing cost are shown before you confirm.

**You'll see:** your balance update immediately after the charge succeeds.

## 3. Recurring bank funding

Link your bank once and approve a pull mandate with a cap you set. Perflo can then top up your balance on a schedule or when your balance runs low, always within your cap, and you can cancel the mandate at any time.

## 4. Crypto deposit

Deposit USDT or USDC directly to your account address. The app shows your deposit address and the supported networks, and standing deposit addresses are available across major networks including Ethereum, Base, Optimism, Polygon, Avalanche, Solana, and Tron.

<Warning>
Always copy your deposit address from inside the app, send only the shown asset on the shown network, and cross-check token contracts on [Networks and contracts](/reference/networks-and-contracts). Deposits sent as the wrong asset or on the wrong network may be unrecoverable.
</Warning>

## Auto-convert on arrival

You can set standing instructions so that incoming money converts automatically. A common setup: a percentage of every salary deposit buys the assets you choose the moment it arrives. See [Assets](/money/assets).

## If something goes wrong

A transfer that hasn't arrived, a declined card top-up, a deposit sent on the wrong network: the most common funding problems are covered step by step in [Getting started troubleshooting](/getting-started/troubleshooting).
