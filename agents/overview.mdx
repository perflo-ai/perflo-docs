Perflo gives AI agents the same money capabilities you have: check balances, buy gold, trade, place prediction positions, manage loans, create cards, send payments, and pay for third-party services per call. Every capability runs inside guardrails you set, and nothing an agent does can exceed them.

## What an agent can do

- **Read:** portfolio, prices, positions, loan health, activity.
- **Money actions:** buy or sell gold and crypto, deposit to Earn, manage loans, open and manage futures positions, place prediction positions, all within your permissions.
- **Spend:** pay whitelisted recipients, fund virtual cards just in time for purchases, and buy pay-per-call services (search, data, media generation, and thousands more) through the x402 protocol.
- **Automate:** run standing rules you describe in plain language, such as "if gold dips 2%, buy $50" or "if my loan health drops below the threshold, add collateral."

## What an agent can never do

- Withdraw to an external address you have not approved. That always requires a fresh confirmation from you.
- Exceed its spending caps or pay a recipient that is not allowlisted.
- Raise its own limits or extend its own expiry.
- Survive the kill switch. One tap stops all agent spending everywhere.

## Where agents run

Perflo agents work wherever you already work with AI:

- **Claude, ChatGPT, and other AI apps** via the Perflo MCP server. Your assistant gains Perflo tools directly in chat.
- **Coding agents and scripts** via the Grant CLI and REST API.
- **Inside the Perflo app** via plain-language automation rules and a daily morning brief.

See [Connect an agent](/agents/connect) to set this up in a few minutes, and [Agent spending and budgets](/agents/spending-and-budgets) for how budgets, the marketplace, and the kill switch work.

<Note>
An agent acts on behalf of a verified person or business. Compliance screening and transaction monitoring apply to agent activity exactly as they do to human activity.
</Note>
