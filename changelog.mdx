## How we communicate changes

- **Everything notable lands here**, newest first: new features, changed behavior, and anything deprecated or removed.
- **Breaking changes get notice.** If a change affects how you or your agents work — a limit, a connector behavior, a supported currency or network — the entry says what changes, when, and what to do about it, before it happens.
- **Migration guides accompany anything that needs action.** An entry that requires you to do something links to a page that walks through it.
