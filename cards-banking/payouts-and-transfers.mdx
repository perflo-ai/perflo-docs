**Goal:** money out of Perflo and into a bank account, mobile wallet, or saved recipient, usually over instant or same-day local rails. You need a verified account with a funded balance.

## Bank payouts worldwide

Send money from your balance to bank accounts and mobile wallets in more than 45 currencies across five regions, settled in the recipient's local currency, mostly on instant or same-day local rails:

| Region | Highlights |
|:---|:---|
| Europe | EUR (SEPA Instant) · GBP (Faster Payments) · DKK · NOK · PLN |
| Americas | USD (FedWire/ACH) · BRL (PIX) · MXN (SPEI) · CAD · COP · CLP and more |
| Africa | NGN · KES (M-Pesa) · GHS · TZS · UGX and more, largely instant mobile money |
| APAC and Middle East | AED · INR (UPI/IMPS) · PHP (InstaPay) · PKR (Raast) · THB (PromptPay) · SGD (FAST) · CNY (AliPay/WeChat Pay) and more |
| Global | USD · EUR · GBP via SWIFT; digital dollar payouts across major networks |

Payouts can be first-party (to your own account) or third-party (to someone else), and both are screened by the compliance program. The full corridor list lives on [Supported currencies and assets](/reference/supported-currencies).

**You'll see:** the payout's status in your activity feed, moving from sent to completed.

## Saved recipients

Save a recipient once (bank details or wallet address) and reuse them safely. Automations and agents can only pay recipients that you have saved and allowlisted. Adding a new recipient always requires you.

## Digital dollar transfers

Send USDT or USDC directly to saved wallet addresses. Transfers are free of network fees for you and arrive on the network shown at send time.

## Asset gifts

Gift an asset to anyone via email or phone number. They claim it with a link after signing up and verifying. See [Assets](/money/assets).

## If something goes wrong

Delayed payouts, rejected transfers, and paused withdrawals are each covered step by step in [Cards and payouts troubleshooting](/cards-banking/troubleshooting).

<Warning>
Payouts to a wrong but valid account number may be unrecoverable. Double-check recipient details; Perflo screens for compliance, but it cannot verify that an account number belongs to the person you intend.
</Warning>
