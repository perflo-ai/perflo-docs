Verified individuals and businesses can open virtual bank accounts with real local account details: an account number and routing details in the local format, in your name. Money sent to these accounts lands in your Perflo balance.

## Live currencies and rails

| Currency | Local rails |
|---|---|
| AED | IPP (instant), FTS |
| USD | ACH, Wire, RTP (US local), SWIFT (global) |
| EUR | SEPA, SEPA Instant |
| GBP | Faster Payments, BACS, CHAPS |
| BRL | PIX, TED |
| MXN | SPEI |
| COP | BRE-B |
| NGN | NIP |
| PHP | InstaPay, PESONet |

More currencies are added over time; the app always shows what is available to you.

## First-party and third-party pay-ins

- **First-party:** move money from your own bank account into Perflo.
- **Third-party:** receive money from other people and companies. Invoice a client in their currency, get paid into your local-details account, and see it in your balance. Businesses use this for customer collections.

## What happens when money arrives

Incoming fiat settles into your cash balance automatically. If you have standing instructions (for example, convert 20% of every deposit to gold), they run on arrival. You get a notification either way.

## Recurring funding from your bank

You can link a bank account once and approve a pull mandate with a cap you set. Perflo can then pull top-ups on a schedule or when your balance runs low, always within your cap. Cancel the mandate anytime.

<Note>
Virtual accounts are provided on regulated rails under Perflo's MSB registration and compliance program. Individuals require KYC and businesses require KYB before accounts are issued.
</Note>
