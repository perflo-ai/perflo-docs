Earn moves idle balance into audited on-chain lending vaults (Morpho vaults) where it is lent out to over-collateralized borrowers. Interest accrues to you continuously.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose an asset">
    Vaults are available for USDT, USDC, and WETH.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deposit">
    Move any amount from your balance into the vault. The current variable APY is shown before you confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch it accrue">
    Your position and earned interest update in the app. Rates float with market supply and demand.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Withdraw anytime">
    Withdraw back to your balance whenever you like, subject to vault liquidity at that moment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Automations that pair well with Earn

- **Idle cash sweep:** automatically move balance above a buffer you set into Earn, and pull it back when you need it.
- **Round-ups:** sweep card round-ups into Earn or gold.

See [AI agents](/agents/overview) for how to set these up in plain language.

## What the yield is, honestly

Yield comes from borrowers paying interest on over-collateralized loans in the vault's markets. It is variable, it is not a bank deposit, and it is not insured. Vault contracts are public and audited, and the exact vault your money sits in is verifiable on-chain. Displayed APY is the current rate, not a promise of future rates.
