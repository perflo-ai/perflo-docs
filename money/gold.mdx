Perflo lets you hold real, allocated physical gold in digital form. Gold on Perflo is XAUT (Tether Gold), where one token represents one troy fine ounce of physical gold held in Swiss vaults by the issuer. You can hold fractions, so you can start small.

## Buying and selling

Buy or sell gold instantly from your cash balance at a live quoted price. The app can display your holding in grams or ounces, alongside a live gold price and historical stats. Conversion executes on-chain through DEX aggregation for best execution; the quote you approve is the price you get.

## Automatic gold buying

Set a standing instruction and Perflo buys gold for you automatically:

- **On deposit:** convert a percentage of every incoming deposit (for example, 20% of your salary) into gold the moment it arrives.
- **On schedule:** buy a fixed amount daily, weekly, or monthly.
- **On rules:** with AI automations, buy on conditions you describe in plain language, such as a price dip. See [AI agents](/agents/overview).

Pause or edit standing instructions at any time.

## Gifting gold

Send gold to anyone with an email address or phone number. The recipient gets a claim link; when they sign up and verify, the gold is theirs. Unclaimed gifts return to you after the claim window shown at send time.

## Borrowing against gold

You do not need to sell gold to unlock cash. You can borrow dollars against it. See [Borrow](/money/borrow).

<Warning>
Gold prices move. Gold can lose value in dollar terms, and holdings are not deposit-insured. Verify the XAUT token contract on [Networks and contracts](/reference/networks-and-contracts).
</Warning>
