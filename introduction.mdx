Perflo is a compliant money platform. It holds a Money Services Business (MSB) registration and gives individuals, businesses, and AI agents regulated access to accounts, cards, trading, and global payments, all from one balance.

Everything runs on public infrastructure you can verify yourself, and your assets sit in an account that only you control. Trading routes to established public venues. Perflo runs no exchange and never takes the other side of your trade.

## Your agent can pay for the tools it needs

Your agent isn't limited to moving your own money. With your permission, it can buy the exact tools it needs to finish a job (fresh data, web search, image and video generation, email, phone calls) and settle up for each one the moment it's used, usually for cents. Every purchase comes out of a budget you set and lands in your activity feed like any other payment. The marketplace holds {marketplaceSize}.

Browse what's available on [The service marketplace](/agents/services), and see [Agent spending and budgets](/agents/spending-and-budgets) for how the limits work.

## Who Perflo is for

**Individuals** open an account with an email address, verify their identity once, and get the full product: cash, assets, trading, cards, and payments.

**Businesses** complete business verification (KYB) and get the same tools plus business bank accounts, third-party pay-ins and payouts, and team-friendly controls.

**AI agents** act on behalf of a verified person or business. An agent never gets your keys. It gets a bounded permission you can revoke at any time, with hard spending caps checked before any money moves.
