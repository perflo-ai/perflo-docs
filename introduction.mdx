Perflo is a compliant money platform for humans and their AI agents. It holds a Money Services Business (MSB) registration and gives individuals, businesses, and the agents that work for them regulated access to accounts, cards, trading, and global payments, all from one balance.

It's also non-custodial: your money sits in an account that only you control, on public infrastructure you can verify yourself. Trading routes to established public venues — Perflo runs no exchange and never takes the other side of your trade.

## What your agent can do with money

Connecting an agent isn't just read-only access to your balance. Inside the budget and limits you set, an agent can buy, trade, pay, and automate on your behalf, and every action lands in your activity feed in real time.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Pay per use for data and tools" icon="database" href="/agents/services">
    Your agent buys exactly what it needs to finish a job (fresh data, web search, image and video generation, email, phone calls) and pays per request, usually cents, over the open x402 pay-per-use standard. No subscriptions, no API keys. The marketplace holds {marketplaceSize}.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Trade prediction markets" icon="square-poll-vertical" href="/trading/predictions">
    A big unlock for agents: take positions on real-world outcomes across elections, sports, economics, and culture on Polymarket, the largest prediction market. Quality filters block thin markets and long-shot bets automatically.

  <Card title="Send payments to people and businesses" icon="paper-plane" href="/cards-banking/payouts-and-transfers">
    Pay individuals and companies directly from your balance in more than 45 currencies, mostly on instant or same-day local rails. Agents can only pay recipients you have saved and allowlisted; adding a new one always requires you.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Build your own automated workflows" icon="arrows-spin" href="/agents/overview">

Every purchase, trade, and payment comes out of a budget you set, and the kill switch stops everything at once. See [Agent spending and budgets](/agents/spending-and-budgets) for how the limits work.

## Who Perflo is for

**Individuals** open an account with an email address, verify their identity once, and get the full product: cash, assets, trading, cards, and payments.

**Businesses** complete business verification (KYB) and get the same tools plus business bank accounts, third-party pay-ins and payouts, and team-friendly controls.

**AI agents** act on behalf of a verified person or business. An agent never gets your keys. It gets a bounded permission you can revoke at any time, with hard spending caps checked before any money moves.
